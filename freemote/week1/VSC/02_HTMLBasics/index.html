Why I chose to join the Freemote program

I have been a software developer for 15+ years. I had always worked with the same technologies all that time. Creating and maintaining billing systems for Blue Cross of Western Pennsylvania using Java, Spring Beans, HTML, JS and JSP, I thought I had it made.

That is, until around 2012. A number of frameworks popped up from Google and Facebook and I broke an important rule: Always keep learning. I had stopped learning, thinking that I would practice the same technologies for the length of my career. If I had only known better!

A company restructuring caused my boss and many of my teammates to leave the company. I stayed but was unable to adapt to the new faces and technologies pouring in as part of Blue Cross of Western Pennsylvania spinoff into a for-profit subsidary, Highmark Health Solutions. Eventually, my position was eliminated. So here I am. Studying new technologies in preparation for that next job opportunity.
